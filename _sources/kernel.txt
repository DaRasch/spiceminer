Kernels
*******

Kernels are files containing information relating to objects in space.

Official documentation
======================
The `NAIF website <https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/toolkit_docs/C/req/kernel.html>`_
has a lot of indepth information about kernels.
